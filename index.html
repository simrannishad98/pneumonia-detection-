About us

Final Year Project based on deep learning

The Final Year Project focuses on utilizing deep learning techniques to enhance the detection of pneumonia through the analysis of X-ray images. Leveraging the power of convolutional neural networks (CNNs), the project aims to develop a robust and accurate model capable of swiftly identifying signs of pneumonia in X-ray scans. By training the model on a large dataset of annotated X-ray images, it will learn to recognize subtle patterns indicative of pneumonia, thereby assisting healthcare professionals in making timely and precise diagnoses. The project not only delves into the technical intricacies of deep learning algorithms but also addresses critical real-world challenges in healthcare by offering a potentially life-saving tool for early detection and treatment of pneumonia.
